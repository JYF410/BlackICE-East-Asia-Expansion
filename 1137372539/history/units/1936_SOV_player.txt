	# Soviet Air Forces underwent a massive reorganization in late 1935 / early 1936. All the brigades were renamed and a lot of new Fast Bomber brigades were created.
	# It makes little sense to use late 1935 / early 1936 OoB, as it was overhauled just then.
	#
	# What I did was taking the 1.01.1937 OoB and removing all the Fast Bomber units from it, providing a "more stable" OoB which IRL existed until mid-1938,
	# when another massive overhaul (introduction of Regiments) took place.
	#
	# I've removed Fast Bomber units, because fast bombers (SB-2) started mass production in 1936 only, and for the next 2 years most of these newly created units had
	# very little of actual bombers, using primarily some biplanes for training.
	#
	# We keep these units commented out rather than deleted, though, so we could make a use of them in the future.
	# Independent detachments and squadrons are often (but not always) consolidated.
